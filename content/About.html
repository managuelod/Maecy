Mission

Our Mission is to provide high-quality education that fosters personal growth, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world.

Vision

We envision a community of learners where students are empowered to reach their full potential and become leaders in their chosen fields. HGBaquiran College aims to be recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching, and impactful community engagement.

Core Values

Dedication, Diligence, Determination, Devotion, and Discipline.
