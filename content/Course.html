Courses


Explore our various programs and choose your path.

Elementary Education

Focuses on teaching young children foundational skills in reading, writing, math, and social studies, with an emphasis on child development and classroom strategies.

College of Criminology

Centers on understanding crime, criminal behavior, and law enforcement, including forensics, psychology, and investigation techniques.

Information Technology

Explores computing systems, software development, cybersecurity, and network management for modern digital solutions.

Hospitality Management

Covers the service industry, focusing on customer service, event planning, and tourism operations in hotels and restaurants.

Business Administration

Delivers expertise in managing organizations effectively, emphasizing marketing, finance, and entrepreneurship.

Secondary Education

Prepares individuals to teach specialized subjects to high school students, incorporating adolescent psychology and effective teaching methods.
